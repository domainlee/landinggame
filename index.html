GamePlay

We wants to build a Metaverse NFT Game world with Rise of Defenders. Here players can build bases, recruit troops and create a kingdom for themselves. Players can also attack, compete with other players with their powerful army.

CAMPAIGN MODE - PVE

Players will go through battles on various maps to get $RDG coins or equipment

TOURNAMENT MODE - PVE RANKING

There are a total of eight categories throughout the tournament for players to conquer, each with an attractive cumulative reward.

ARENA MODE - PVE RANKING

Arena is a special Mode, where players use their existing Hero system to fight other players for their glory and reward.
