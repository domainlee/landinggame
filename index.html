GamePlay

Apero wants to build a Metaverse NFT Game world with Rise of Defenders. Here players can build bases, recruit troops and create a kingdom for themselves. Players can also attack, compete with other players with their powerful army.

CAMPAIGN MODE

Players will go through battles on various maps and fight with different enemies to get many attractive rewards from loot boxes such as tokens for games that are RDG coins or equipment. Each game will require the player to spend a certain number of stamina to enter. The higher stage that players reach, the stronger the enemies they have to face, and of course, the rewards will be worth the efforts that players put in.

TOURNAMENT MODE

In the Tournament Mode, all players will fight in the same endless game. The more monsters they destroy, the higher the player's score and also the higher the ranking. At the end of the season, the system will sum up the points to send reward to players and changing the rank based on their score. In tournament, players can even earn RDR and Premium Loot Box if their rank is high enough.

ARENA MODE

Arena is a special mode, where players use their existing heroes to fight other players for their glory and reward. In this mode, each player is allowed to bring in up to three heroes and the fights will take place between two players on a turn-based mechanic. Similar to Tournament mode, Arena mode also has a ranking system and rewards RDR for players in certain ranks.
